Privacy Policy / プライバシーポリシー

Language:

Last updated: 2025-08-31

JewelSort respects your privacy.

Data Collection

  • The app does not collect personal information or analytics.
  • Game progress (e.g., streak, best records, settings) is stored locally on your device (UserDefaults).

Purchases

In‑app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive payment information.

Network Access

The app is designed to work offline and does not make network requests during normal gameplay.

Contact

For questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or email nekomura@liz-aria.com.
